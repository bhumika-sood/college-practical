Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering of Mahatma Gandhi Mission College of Engineering & Technology, Noida. The Department was established in 2000, offers Undergraduate program (B. Tech) Mechanical engineering with an intake of 120. The department has good infrastructure and is equipped with full-fledged laboratories such as CAD/CAM lab, Fluid Mechanics Lab, Refrigeration Lab, Engineering Mechanics lab, Thermodynamics Lab, etc.
The department is having well qualified and experienced faculty members. Faculty motivates and guides the students to do projects related to Advanced Technologies in Mechanical Engineering.
Audiovisual classes are conducted as per Time-Table to gain the information regarding latest technologies in the field of Mechanical Engineering.
Emphasis is given on communication skills, creativity, leadership, and innovation to make our students capable of undertaking responsibilities. Our goal is to provide students with a balance of intellectual and practical experiences that enable them to serve a variety of societal needs. Department has established Students Society of Mechanical Engineering (INNOVENT) for giving scope for the hidden potentials existing amongst the students.
Do visit our website for more details and feel free to approach us for any query.
